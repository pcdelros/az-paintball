Planning flow

Ask for the right details up front.

A good event request should collect enough information to respond helpfully without asking visitors for unnecessary personal data.

  1. 1

    Choose a group type

    Birthday, corporate outing, school group, private match, open play, or tournament-style session.

  2. 2

    Estimate player count

    Separate confirmed players from possible players so staff can plan rental gear and staffing.

  3. 3

    Pick date options

    Ask for first choice, backup date, and preferred time window.

  4. 4

    Confirm next steps

    Send accurate pricing, waiver details, arrival instructions, and payment requirements.

Trust and safety

Use practical event copy.

Include before launch

  • Real package names and inclusions
  • Minimum and maximum group size
  • Refund, deposit, and weather policies
  • Arrival and waiver requirements

Avoid

  • Fake discounts or fake availability
  • Unverified tournament dates
  • Collecting sensitive information too early
  • Forms that appear to submit but do nothing
