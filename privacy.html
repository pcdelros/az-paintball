Privacy and security

Privacy and form notes for this static demo site.

This page explains the current demo behavior and what should be added before the website collects real visitor information.

Current demo behavior

  • No analytics or tracking scripts are included.
  • The contact form does not send, store, or email information.
  • No API keys, passwords, tokens, or private URLs are included.
  • No third-party CDN scripts are required.

Before real launch

  • Connect the form to a trusted form service or backend.
  • Add server-side validation and spam protection.
  • Publish a real privacy policy that matches the data being collected.
  • Update contact details, sitemap, and domain settings.
