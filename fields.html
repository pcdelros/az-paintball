Local SEO checklist

Make every location page helpful first.

Good location content

  • Real name, address, phone, and hours
  • Specific game formats and rental details
  • Original photos or approved media
  • Helpful driving, parking, and arrival notes

Avoid weak SEO content

  • Fake locations or unverified claims
  • Duplicate city pages with swapped names
  • Keyword stuffing or doorway pages
  • Fake reviews, prices, dates, or statistics
