Beginner guide

Simple paintball tips for first-time Arizona players.

Help new visitors show up prepared, comfortable, and ready to follow the field rules.

What to wear

Wear comfortable clothes that can get dirty. Long sleeves, athletic pants, and closed-toe shoes are usually a good starting point.

Arizona note: add real field-specific heat, shade, and hydration guidance after confirming your location rules.

What to bring

  • Water bottle
  • Photo ID if required
  • Waiver confirmation if completed online
  • Change of clothes or towel for the ride home

Safety basics

Always keep your mask on in active play areas, listen to refs, keep barrel covers on outside active play, and ask questions before the first round starts.

First-game mindset

Stay low, communicate with teammates, keep moving, and do not worry about being perfect. A good first game is about learning the flow and having fun safely.
